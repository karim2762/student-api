Student Records Portal

Four things worth knowing first

A plain-language walkthrough of how this portal works, what it does to protect student data, and where to go if something's wrong.

01 · How it works

Search by roll number, name, or place

An exact roll number like Y24CS3201 jumps straight to that record. A name, place, gender, or category instead returns a browsable list.

02 · Privacy & disclaimer

Sensitive fields stay hidden until you confirm

Aadhaar, mobile, and date of birth are blurred by default. Revealing one confirms you're authorised — please don't screenshot or share outside official use.

03 · Data accuracy

Records can lag behind recent changes

Information comes from institutional admission data, so very recent updates may not show yet. Spotted something wrong? Flag it via the contact below.

04 · Need access or help?

The admin is one message away

Access requests, corrections, or general questions all go to the same place — message the admin directly on Instagram or Telegram.
