About
Worth a minute before you search

Four things worth knowing first

A short, plain-language walkthrough of how this portal works, what it does to protect student data, and where to go if something's wrong.

How it works

Search by roll number, name, or place

An exact roll number like Y24CS3201 jumps straight to that record. A name, place, gender, or category instead returns a list you can browse and open.

Privacy & disclaimer

Sensitive fields stay hidden until you confirm

Aadhaar number, mobile, and date of birth are blurred by default. Revealing one means you're confirming you're authorised to see it — please don't copy, screenshot, or share it outside official use.

Data accuracy

Records can lag behind recent changes

Information comes from institutional admission data, so very recent updates may not show yet. Spotted something that looks wrong or outdated? Flag it via the contact section.

Need access or help?

The admin is one message away

Access requests, corrections, or general questions about a record all go to the same place — message the admin directly on Instagram or Telegram.
